解析函数

Posted on 2023-08-17 In 复变

Complex numbers \(\mathbb{C}\)

\[ (a,b)\quad a,b\in \mathbb{R} \]

Define operations \(+,\cdot\)

  1. \((a,b)+(c,d)=(a+c,b+d)\)
  2. \((a,b)\cdot (c,d)=(ac-bd,ad+bc)\)

\(\therefore \mathbb{C}\)形成一个 field

\[ \mathbb{R}\quad a\in \mathbb{R} \]

\[ a\mapsto (a,0) \]

\[ (a,0)\cdot (c,d)=(ac,ad) \]

\[ a(c,d)=(ac,ad) \]

\[ (0,1)\cdot (0,1)=(0-1,0)=(-1,0) \]

denote \((0,1)=i\), \(i^2=-1\), \(x^2+1=0\).

\[ z\in \mathbb{C}\rightarrow z=a+ib \]

\[ \begin{aligned} z&=(a,b)\\ &=(a,0)+(0,b)\\ &=a+b(0,1)\\ &=a+ib \end{aligned} \]

\(a=\Re z\), real part

\(b=\Im z\), imaginary part

\(|z|=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\), modulus

\(\bar{z}=a-ib\), complex conjugate

\[ z,w\in \mathbb{C} \]

Define \[ d(z,w)=|z-w| \]

Thus: \(z,w\in \mathbb{C}\)

\[ |z+w|\leqslant |z|+|w| \]

\((\mathbb{C},d)\): metric space

open ball centered at \(z\) with radius \(r\)

\[ B(z;r)=\{w\in \mathbb{C}\big | |w-z|<r\} \]

\[ f: \Omega\rightarrow \mathbb{C}\quad \Omega\subset \mathbb{C} \]

\(\Omega\) 是 \(\mathbb{C}\) 的一个(connected open) domain，现在在\(\Omega\)上选一个点

\[ z_0\in \Omega \]

\(f\) is said to be differentiable at \(z_0\), if \(\displaystyle{\lim_{z\rightarrow z_0}\frac{f(z)-f(z_0)}{z-z_0}}\) exists, denoted by \(f'(z_0)\)

If \(f\) is diff. at every point of \(\Omega\), then we say \(f\) is a holomorphic function （解析函数/全纯函数） on \(\Omega\).

\[ O(\Omega)=\{\text{all of the holomorphic functions on }\Omega \} \]

有\(S\subset \mathbb{C}\) set, \(f\in O(S)\) means, \(f\) is holomorphic on some open neighborhood of \(S\).

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(z_0)=\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}(z_0) \]

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}(z_0)=-\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}(z_0) \]

Cauchy-Riemann eq.

可导 \(\Rightarrow\) 柯西黎曼方程，但柯西黎曼方程 \(\nRightarrow\) 可导

例.

\[ u(x,y)=\begin{cases} \displaystyle{\frac{x^3-y^3}{x^2+y^2}} & (x,y)\neq (0,0)\\ 0 & (x,y)= (0,0) \end{cases} \]

\[ v(x,y)=\begin{cases} \displaystyle{\frac{x^3+y^3}{x^2+y^2}} & (x,y)\neq (0,0)\\ 0 & (x,y)= (0,0) \end{cases} \]

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(0,0)=1, \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}(0,0)=-1 \]

\[ \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}(0,0)=1, \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}(0,0)=1 \]

满足柯西黎曼方程

along \(x\)-axis \[ \lim \frac{f(x,0)-f(0,0)}{x}=\frac{x+ix-0}{x}=1+i \]

along \(x=y\) \[ \lim \frac{f(x,x)-f(0,0)}{x+ix}=\frac{ix}{x+ix}=\frac{i}{1+i}=\frac{1+i}{2} \]

If \(f\in C^1(\Omega)\)

Set Cauchy-Riemann operator

\[ \frac{\partial}{\partial \bar{z}}=\frac 12\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right) \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}} & = \frac 12 \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)(u+iv) \\ &= \frac 12\left (\left (\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\right)+i\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\right) \right)\\ &=0 \end{aligned} \]

在满足柯西黎曼方程的情况下

\[ \Leftrightarrow f\in O(\Omega) \]

复变这门学问就是在研究这个方程式： \[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial \bar{z}}=g \]

\(g=0\)时，是解析函数

讲点微积分。

Curve

\[ \gamma: [a,b]\rightarrow \mathbb{C} \]

\[ t\mapsto \gamma(t)=(x(t),y(t)) \]

应该是 \(t\in [a,b],\ \gamma(t)\in \mathbb{C}\)

Smooth curve \[ \gamma \in C^1([a,b]), \ and \ \ \gamma'(t)\neq 0\ \ on\ \ (a,b) \]

Piecewise smooth \[ \gamma: [a,b]\rightarrow \mathbb{C} \]

\[ \exists P=\{a=t_0<t_1<\cdots <t_{n-1}<t_n=b\} \]

\[ s.t. \quad \gamma \text{ is smooth on each }[t_{j-1},t_j], 1\leqslant j\leqslant n \]

Theo (Green)

\(D\) is a bounded domain in \(\mathbb{R}^2\) with piecewise smooth boundary \(\partial D\), let \(P,Q\) be two functions which is \(C^1\) in some open neighborhood of \(\bar{D}\). Assume \(\partial D\) is positively oriented. (沿边界走时domain在左边)

\(\bar{D}=cl (D)\), closure of \(D\)

Then

\[ \oint_C Pdx+Qdy=\iint_D \left(\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y} \right)dxdy \]

Theo. Let \(D\) be a simply connected domain in \(\mathbb{C}\)

Suppose \(f\in C^1(D)\), then \[ f\in O(D)\Leftrightarrow \oint_{\tau} f(z)dz=0 \]

for every piecewise smooth simple closed curve \(\tau\) in \(D\)

simply connected 说明两件事 1. arcwise connected： 任何两个点都能用arc连起来 2. \(\pi_1(D)=0\)： domain内每个封闭曲线都能缩成一个点（没有洞）

simple：曲线除了起点和终点都不会自我相交

closed：起点和终点是同一个点

\(\Omega\): region surrounded by \(\Gamma\).

\[ \begin{aligned} \oint_{\Gamma} f(z)dz & = \oint_{\Gamma}f(z)(dx+idy) \\ & = \iint_{\Omega}\left(i\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right)dxdy \\ & = i\iint_{\Omega}\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right)dxdy \\ & = 2i\iint_{\Omega}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}dxdy \end{aligned} \]

\[ \oint_{\Gamma} f(z)dz=0\Leftrightarrow \frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}=0\ on\ D.\ \blacksquare \]
