奇異點

發表於 2023-08-22 分類於 複變

Singular point 奇異點

  1. \(\exists M>0, |f(z)|\leqslant M, \forall z\in D/\{z_0\}\) （removeable singularity）
  2. \(\displaystyle \lim_{z\rightarrow z_0}|f(z)|=+\infty\) （pole 極點）
  3. \(f\) is not bounded on \(D/\{z_0\}\) and \(\displaystyle \lim_{z\rightarrow z_0}|f(z)|\neq +\infty\) （esential singularity 本質奇異點）最複雜
