Lecture 1

Complex numbers \(\mathbb{C}\)

\[ (a,b)\quad a,b\in \mathbb{R} \]

Define operations \(+,\cdot\)

  1. \((a,b)+(c,d)=(a+c,b+d)\)
  2. \((a,b)\cdot (c,d)=(ac-bd,ad+bc)\)

\(\therefore \mathbb{C}\)形成一个 field

\[ \mathbb{R}\quad a\in \mathbb{R} \]

\[ a\mapsto (a,0) \]

\[ (a,0)\cdot (c,d)=(ac,ad) \]

\[ a(c,d)=(ac,ad) \]

\[ (0,1)\cdot (0,1)=(0-1,0)=(-1,0) \]

denote \((0,1)=i\), \(i^2=-1\), \(x^2+1=0\).

\[ z\in \mathbb{C}\rightarrow z=a+ib \]

\[ \begin{aligned} z&=(a,b)\\ &=(a,0)+(0,b)\\ &=a+b(0,1)\\ &=a+ib \end{aligned} \]

\(a=\Re z\), real part

\(b=\Im z\), imaginary part

\(|z|=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\), modulus

\(\bar{z}=a-ib\), complex conjugate

\[ z,w\in \mathbb{C} \]

Define \[ d(z,w)=|z-w| \]

Thus: \(z,w\in \mathbb{C}\)

\[ |z+w|\leqslant |z|+|w| \]

\((\mathbb{C},d)\): metric space

open ball centered at \(z\) with radius \(r\)

\[ B(z;r)=\{w\in \mathbb{C}\big | |w-z|<r\} \]

\[ f: \Omega\rightarrow \mathbb{C}\quad \Omega\subset \mathbb{C} \]

(connected open) domain

\[ z_0\in \Omega \]

\(f\) is said to be differentiable at \(z_0\), if \(\displaystyle{\lim_{z\rightarrow z_0}\frac{f(z)-f(z_0)}{z-z_0}}\) exists, denoted by \(f'(z_0)\)

If \(f\) is diff. at every point of \(\Omega\), then we say \(f\) is a helomorphic function （解析函数/全纯函数） on \(\Omega\).

\[ O(z)=\{\text{all of the helomorphic functions on }\Omega \} \]

有\(S\subset \mathbb{C}\) set, \(f\in O(S)\) means, \(f\) is helomorphic on some open neighborhood of \(S\).

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(z_0)=\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}(z_0) \]

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}(z_0)=-\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}(z_0) \]

Cauchy-Riemann eq.

可导 \(\Rightarrow\) 柯西黎曼方程，但柯西黎曼方程 \(\nRightarrow\) 可导

例.

\[ u(x,y)=\begin{cases} \displaystyle{\frac{x^3-y^3}{x^2+y^2}} & (x,y)\neq (0,0)\\ 0 & (x,y)= (0,0) \end{cases} \]

\[ v(x,y)=\begin{cases} \displaystyle{\frac{x^3+y^3}{x^2+y^2}} & (x,y)\neq (0,0)\\ 0 & (x,y)= (0,0) \end{cases} \]

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(0,0)=1, \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}(0,0)=-1 \]

\[ \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}(0,0)=1, \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}(0,0)=1 \]

满足柯西黎曼方程

along \(x\)-axis \[ \lim \frac{f(x,0)-f(0,0)}{x}=\frac{x+ix-0}{x}=1+i \]

along \(x=y\) \[ \lim \frac{f(x,x)-f(0,0)}{x+ix}=\frac{ix}{x+ix}=\frac{i}{1+i}=\frac{1+i}{2} \]
