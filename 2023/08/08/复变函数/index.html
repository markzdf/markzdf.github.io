Complex numbers \(\mathbb{C}\)

Define operations \(+,\cdot\)

  1. \((a,b)+(c,d)=(a+c,b+d)\)
  2. \((a,b)\cdot (c,d)=(ac-bd,ad+bc)\)

\(\therefore \mathbb{C}\)形成一个 field

\[ \mathbb{R}\quad a\in \mathbb{R} \]

\[ a\mapsto (a,0) \]

denote \((0,1)=i\), \(i^2=-1\), \(x^2+1=0\).

\[ z\in \mathbb{C}\rightarrow z=a+ib \]

\[ \begin{aligned} z&=(a,b)\\ &=(a,0)+(0,b)\\ &=a+b(0,1)\\ &=a+ib \end{aligned} \]

\(a=\Re z\), real part

\(b=\Im z\), imaginary part

\(|z|=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\), modulus

\(\bar{z}=a-ib\), complex conjugate

\[ z,w\in \mathbb{C} \]

\[ |z+w|\leqslant |z|+|w| \]

\((\mathbb{C},d)\): metric space

open ball centered at \(z\) with radius \(r\)

\[ B(z;r)=\{w\in \mathbb{C}\big | |w-z|<r\} \]

\[ f: \Omega\rightarrow \mathbb{C}\quad \Omega\subset \mathbb{C} \]

\(\Omega\) 是 \(\mathbb{C}\) 的一个(connected open) domain，现在在\(\Omega\)上选一个点

\[ z_0\in \Omega \]

\(f\) is said to be differentiable at \(z_0\), if \(\displaystyle{\lim_{z\rightarrow z_0}\frac{f(z)-f(z_0)}{z-z_0}}\) exists, denoted by \(f'(z_0)\)

If \(f\) is diff. at every point of \(\Omega\), then we say \(f\) is a holomorphic function （解析函数/全纯函数） on \(\Omega\).

\[ O(\Omega)=\{\text{all of the holomorphic functions on }\Omega \} \]

有\(S\subset \mathbb{C}\) set, \(f\in O(S)\) means, \(f\) is holomorphic on some open neighborhood of \(S\).

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(z_0)=\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}(z_0) \]

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}(z_0)=-\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}(z_0) \]

Cauchy-Riemann eq.

可导 \(\Rightarrow\) 柯西黎曼方程，但柯西黎曼方程 \(\nRightarrow\) 可导

例.

\[ u(x,y)=\begin{cases} \displaystyle{\frac{x^3-y^3}{x^2+y^2}} & (x,y)\neq (0,0)\\ 0 & (x,y)= (0,0) \end{cases} \]

\[ v(x,y)=\begin{cases} \displaystyle{\frac{x^3+y^3}{x^2+y^2}} & (x,y)\neq (0,0)\\ 0 & (x,y)= (0,0) \end{cases} \]

\[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(0,0)=1, \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}(0,0)=-1 \]

\[ \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}(0,0)=1, \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}(0,0)=1 \]

满足柯西黎曼方程

along \(x\)-axis \[ \lim \frac{f(x,0)-f(0,0)}{x}=\frac{x+ix-0}{x}=1+i \]

along \(x=y\) \[ \lim \frac{f(x,x)-f(0,0)}{x+ix}=\frac{ix}{x+ix}=\frac{i}{1+i}=\frac{1+i}{2} \]

If \(f\in C^1(\Omega)\)

Set Cauchy-Riemann operator

\[ \frac{\partial}{\partial \bar{z}}=\frac 12\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right) \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}} & = \frac 12 \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial}{\partial y} \right)(u+iv) \\ &= \frac 12\left (\left (\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\right)+i\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\right) \right)\\ &=0 \end{aligned} \]

在满足柯西黎曼方程的情况下

\[ \Leftrightarrow f\in O(\Omega) \]

复变这门学问就是在研究这个方程式： \[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial \bar{z}}=g \]

\(g=0\)时，是解析函数

Theo (Green)

\(D\) is a bounded domain in \(\mathbb{R}^2\) with piecewise smooth boundary \(\partial D\), let \(P,Q\) be two functions which is \(C^1\) in some open neighborhood of \(\bar{D}\). Assume \(\partial D\) is positively oriented. (沿边界走时domain在左边)

\(\bar{D}=cl (D)\), closure of \(D\)

Then

\[ \oint_C Pdx+Qdy=\iint_D \left(\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y} \right)dxdy \]

Theo. Let \(D\) be a simply connected domain in \(\mathbb{C}\)

Suppose \(f\in C^1(D)\), then \[ f\in O(D)\Leftrightarrow \oint_{\tau} f(z)dz=0 \]

for every piecewise smooth simple closed curve \(\tau\) in \(D\)

simply connected 说明两件事 1. arcwise connected： 任何两个点都能用arc连起来 2. \(\pi_1(D)=0\)： domain内每个封闭曲线都能缩成一个点（没有洞）

simple：曲线除了起点和终点都不会自我相交

closed：起点和终点是同一个点

\(\Omega\): region surrounded by \(\Gamma\).

\[ \begin{aligned} \oint_{\Gamma} f(z)dz & = \oint_{\Gamma}f(z)(dx+idy) \\ & = \iint_{\Omega}\left(i\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right)dxdy \\ & = i\iint_{\Omega}\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right)dxdy \\ & = 2i\iint_{\Omega}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}dxdy \end{aligned} \]

\[ \oint_{\Gamma} f(z)dz=0\Leftrightarrow \frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}=0\ on\ D.\ \blacksquare \]

Theo : (Cauchy's integral formula) Let \(D\) be a bounded domain in \(\mathbb{C}\) with piecewise smooth doundary. Suppose \(f\in C^1(\bar{D})\), Then \[ f(w)=\frac{1}{2\pi i}\oint_{\partial D}\frac{f(z)}{z-w}dw-\frac{1}{\pi}\iint_D \frac{\displaystyle{\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}}(z)}{z-w}dxdy,\quad \forall w\in D \]

where \(z=x+iy\).

consider \(D_{\varepsilon}=D/\bar{B_{\varepsilon}}\), \(B_{\epsilon}=B(w;\epsilon)\)

挖掉圆盘后有内外两个边界，曲线积分等于外围的减内围的

\[ \begin{aligned} \oint_{\partial D_{\epsilon}}\frac{f(z)}{z-w}dz&=2i\iint_{D_{\epsilon}}\frac{\partial}{\partial \bar{z}}\left(\frac{f(z)}{z-w}\right)dxdy \\ &=2i\iint_{D_{\epsilon}}\frac{\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}(z)}{z-w}dxdy \quad \star \end{aligned} \]

\[ \oint_{\partial D_{\epsilon}}\frac{f(z)}{z-w}dz=\oint_{\partial D}\frac{f(z)}{z-w}dz-\oint_{\partial B_{\epsilon}}\frac{f(z)}{z-w}dz \quad \star \]

set \(z=w+\epsilon e^{i\theta}\), \(z-w=\epsilon e^{i\theta}\)

\[ \begin{aligned} \oint_{\partial B_{\epsilon}}\frac{f(z)}{z-w}dz&=\oint_{\partial B_{\epsilon}}\frac{f(w+\epsilon e^{i\theta})}{\epsilon e^{i\theta}}dz\\ &=\int_0^{2\pi} \frac{f(w+\epsilon e^{i\theta})}{\epsilon e^{i\theta}}i\epsilon e^{i\theta}d\theta\\ &=i\int_0^{2\pi}f(w+\epsilon e^{i\theta})d\theta\\ &\xrightarrow{\epsilon\rightarrow0} i\int_0^{2\pi}f(w)d\theta\\ &=2\pi if(w) \end{aligned} \]

下面考虑这个积分

\[ 2i\iint_{D_{\epsilon}}\frac{\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}(z)}{z-w}dxdy \]

因为\(f\)是\(C^1\)函数，\(\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}(z)\in C(\bar{D})\)

K: compact

因为会收敛(用极坐标)

\[ \iint_B\frac{1}{|z|}dxdy=\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_0^R\frac 1r rdrd\theta \]

\[ 2i\iint_{D_{\epsilon}}\frac{\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}(z)}{z-w}dxdy\rightarrow 2i\iint_{D}\frac{\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}}(z)}{z-w}dxdy \]

Theo (Cauchys)

Let \(D\) be a simply connected domain in \(\mathbb{C}\),

\(C\): simple closed curve in \(D\)

If \(f\in O(D)\). Then

\[ \oint_C f(z)dz=0 \]

之前有\(C^1\)是为了诉诸于格林定理，但证明这个定理不能用格林，要用完全不同的想法

\(R\): rectangle

\(L\): length of \(\partial R\)

Claim: \(\oint_{\partial R}f(z)dz=0\)

反证法

Assume \(\exists R\) s.t. \[ \oint_{\partial R}f(z)dz=A\neq 0 \]

切割，对大长方形积一次，相当于对四个小长方形各积一次

\[ \oint_Rf(z)dz=\sum_{k=1}^4\oint_{R^{(k)}}f(z)dz \]

\[ \therefore \exists R_1=R^{(j)} \ \text{some }j, \quad s.t. \bigg|\oint_{\partial R_1}f(z)dz \bigg|\geq \frac{A}{4} \]

\[ \bigg|\oint_{\partial R_2}f(z)dz \bigg|\geq \frac{A}{4^2} \]

obtain a seq. of rectangle \(\{R_n\}\)

\[ \bigg|\oint_{\partial R_n}f(z)dz \bigg|\geq \frac{A}{4^n} \]

By Cantor intersection theorem \[ \exists P_0\in R_n, \ \forall n\quad P_0\in D \]

Write near \(P_0\)

\[ f(z)=f(P_0)+f'(P_0)(z-P_0)+\epsilon (z)(z-P_0) \]

\(\epsilon\rightarrow 0\) as \(z\rightarrow P_0\)

n 很大时，\(f(z)\)可以用\(f(P_0)+f'(P_0)(z-P_0)+\epsilon (z)(z-P_0)\)替换

\[ \oint_{\partial R_n}\left[f(P_0)+f'(P_0)(z-P_0)+\epsilon (z)(z-P_0)\right]dz \]

多项式一定是某个函数的微分， \[ \therefore f(P_0)+f'(P_0)(z-P_0)=Q'(z) \]

\(Q(z)\): polynomial of degree at most 2

\(z=\gamma(t)\)

\[ \begin{aligned} &\oint_{\partial R_n}[f(P_0)+f'(P_0)(z-P_0)]dz\\ =&\oint_{\partial R_n}Q'(z)dz\\ =&\int_a^bQ'(z)\gamma'(t)dt\\ =&\int_a^bd(Q(z))\\ =& Q(\gamma(b))-Q(\gamma(a))=0\quad \text{首尾相连} \end{aligned} \]

因为 \[ |\oint_{\partial R_n}dz|=|\int_a^b\gamma'(t)dt|\leq \int_a^b|\gamma'(t)|dt=Ln \] \((z-P_0)\)小于等于\(Ln\)（弦长小于等于直径），\(dz\)积分也小于\(Ln\)，所以两次。\(\epsilon\)用最大值提出来 \[ \begin{aligned} &|\oint_{\partial R_n}\left[f(P_0)+f'(P_0)(z-P_0)+\epsilon (z)(z-P_0)\right]dz|\\ =&|\oint_{\partial R_n}\epsilon (z)(z-P_0)dz|\\ \leq &\max_{z\in \partial R_n}|\epsilon(z)|\cdot L_n^2 \end{aligned} \]

由于 \(\displaystyle{L_n=\frac{L}{2^n}}\)

\[ \max_{z\in \partial R_n}|\epsilon(z)|\cdot \left(\frac{L}{2^n}\right)^2\geq \frac{|A|}{4^n} \]

当\(n\rightarrow \infty\)时，\(\max_{z\in \partial R_n}|\epsilon(z)|\rightarrow 0\)

\[ \therefore \max_{z\in \partial R_n}|\epsilon(z)|\geq \frac{|A|}{L^2}\Rightarrow 0\geq \frac{|A|}{L^2}\quad \text{矛盾}.\ \square \]

目前证得在所有的长方形上 \[ \oint_{\partial R}f(z)dz=0 \]
