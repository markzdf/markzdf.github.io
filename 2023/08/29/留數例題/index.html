留數例題

發表於 2023-08-29 更新於 2023-08-31 分類於 複變 閱讀次數：

Contour integral（路徑積分）

例題1

計算 \[ \int_{-\pi}^{\pi}\frac{d\theta}{5+3\cos \theta} \]

微積分做法：let \(u=\tan \frac{\theta}{2}\cdots\)

留數做法

\[ z=e^{i\theta}=\cos\theta+i\sin\theta \]

\[ \bar{z}=e^{-i\theta}=\cos\theta-i\sin\theta \]

\(|z|=1\), \(\bar{z}=\frac{1}{z}\)

\[ \cos\theta=\frac{1}{2}(z+\bar{z})=\frac{1}{2}(z+\frac{1}{z}) \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi}\frac{d\theta}{5+3\cos \theta}&=\int_C \frac{dz}{iz(5+\frac{3}{2}(z+\frac{1}{z}))}\\ &=\frac{1}{i}\int_C\frac{dz}{\frac{3}{2}z^2+5z+\frac{3}{2}}\\ &=\frac{2}{i}\int_C \frac{dz}{3z^2+10z+3}\\ &=\frac{2}{i}\int_C\frac{dz}{(3z+1)(z+3)}\\ &=4\pi \cdot \underbrace{\frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C\frac{dz}{(3z+1)(z+3)}}_{Res\left(\frac{1}{(3z+1)(z+3)};-\frac{1}{3}\right)} \end{aligned} \]

\(\frac{1}{(3z+1)(z+3)}\)有兩個simple pole，分別在\(z=-3,-\frac{1}{3}\)，因為單位圓半徑是1，\(z=-3\)在積分範圍外，只剩下\(-\frac{1}{3}\)

\[ Res=\lim_{z\rightarrow -\frac{1}{3}}(z+\frac{1}{3})\frac{1}{(3z+1)(z+3)}=\lim_{z\rightarrow -\frac{1}{3}}\frac{1}{3z+9}=\frac{1}{8} \]

\[ \therefore \int_{-\pi}^{\pi}\frac{d\theta}{5+3\cos \theta}=\frac{4\pi}{8}=\frac{\pi}{2} \]

例題2

計算 \[ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{P(x)}{Q(x)}dx \]

條件：\(P,Q\) real-valued polynomials, s.t.

  1. \(Q(x)\neq 0, x\in \mathbb{R}\)
  2. \(\deg Q\geqslant 2+\deg P\)

例題2.1

\[ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{dx}{1+x^4} \]

微積分方法：

\[ x^4+1=x^4+2x^2+1-2x^2=(x^2+1)^2-2x^2=(x^2+\sqrt{2}x+1)(x^2-\sqrt{2}x+1) \]

留數方法：

方法一

畫半圓

先算奇異點\(z^4+1=0\Rightarrow z^4=-1=e^{\pi i}\)

\[ z=e^{\frac{\pi}{4}i}, e^{\frac{3}{4}\pi i}, e^{\frac{5}{4}\pi i}, e^{\frac{7}{4}\pi i} \]

\[ \int_{\Gamma}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}=2\pi i Res\left(\frac{1}{1+z^4};e^{\frac{\pi}{4}i},e^{\frac{3}{4}\pi i}\right) \]

\[ Res\ at\ e^{\frac{\pi}{4}i} =\lim_{z\rightarrow e^{\frac{\pi}{4}i}}(z-e^{\frac{\pi}{4}i})\frac{1}{z^4+1} =\lim_{z\rightarrow e^{\frac{\pi}{4}i}}\frac{1}{\frac{z^4+1-0}{z-e^{\frac{\pi}{4}i}}} =\frac{1}{(z^4+1)'\vert_{z=e^{\frac{\pi}{4}i}}} =\frac{1}{4e^{\frac{3}{4}\pi i}} =\frac{1}{4}e^{-\frac{3}{4}\pi i} =\frac{1}{4}(\cos \frac{3}{4}\pi-i\sin \frac{3}{4}\pi) \]

\[ Res\ at\ e^{\frac{3\pi}{4}i} =\lim_{z\rightarrow e^{\frac{3\pi}{4}i}}(z-e^{\frac{3\pi}{4}i})\frac{1}{z^4+1} =\lim_{z\rightarrow e^{\frac{3\pi}{4}i}}\frac{1}{\frac{z^4+1-0}{z-e^{\frac{3\pi}{4}i}}} =\frac{1}{(z^4+1)'\vert_{z=e^{\frac{3\pi}{4}i}}} =\frac{1}{4e^{\frac{9}{4}\pi i}} =\frac{1}{4}e^{-\frac{9}{4}\pi i} =\frac{1}{4}e^{-\frac{\pi}{4} i} =\frac{1}{4}(\cos \frac{1}{4}\pi-i\sin \frac{1}{4}\pi) \]

\(e^{-\frac{9}{4}\pi i}=e^{-\frac{\pi}{4} i}\)是因為\(e^{2\pi i}=1\)，把兩個點的留數相加

\[ \therefore \int_{\Gamma}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}=2\pi i\times \frac{1}{4}(-2i\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2})=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\pi \]

\[ \int_{-R}^R\frac{dx}{1+x^4}+\int_{\Gamma_R}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\pi \]

下面證明\(R\rightarrow \infty\)時，第二項趨近於0

\[ \lim_{R\rightarrow\infty}\left|\int_{\Gamma_R}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}\right|\leqslant \lim_{R\rightarrow\infty}\int_0^{\pi}\frac{Rd\theta}{|1+z^4|}\leqslant \lim_{R\rightarrow\infty}\int_0^{\pi}\frac{2Rd\theta}{R^4}=\lim_{R\rightarrow\infty}\frac{2\pi }{R}=0 \]

where \(|1+z^4|\geqslant \frac{|z^4|}{2}=\frac{R^4}{2}\)

方法二

畫四分之一圓

\[ \int_{\Gamma}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}=2\pi i\cdot Res\left(\frac{1}{1+z^4};e^{\frac{1}{4}\pi i}\right)=2\pi i\cdot \frac{1}{4}e^{-\frac{3}{4}\pi i}=\frac{2\pi i}{4}(\cos \frac{3}{4}\pi-i\sin \frac{3}{4}\pi)=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{4}\pi (1-i) \]

\[ \int_{\Gamma}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}=\int_{\Gamma_1}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}+\int_{\Gamma_3}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}+\int_{\Gamma_R}\frac{dz}{1+z^4} \]

\[ \Downarrow \]

\[ \int_0^R\frac{dx}{1+x^4}+\int_R^0\frac{idy}{1+y^4}+\underbrace{\int_{\Gamma_R}\frac{dz}{1+z^4}}_{\xrightarrow{R\rightarrow \infty}0}=(1-i)\int_0^R\frac{dx}{1+x^4}\xrightarrow{R\rightarrow \infty}(1-i)\int_0^{\infty}\frac{dx}{1+x^4} \]

\[ \because (1-i)\int_0^{\infty}\frac{dx}{1+x^4}=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{4}\pi (1-i) \]

\[ \therefore \int_0^{\infty}\frac{dx}{1+x^4}=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{4}\pi \]
