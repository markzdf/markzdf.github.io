公司金融

發表於 2023-10-06 更新於 2023-10-08 分類於 课程 ， 其他 閱讀次數：

这里我想弄一个公司金融cheatsheet，整理一些公式，它们都很简单但很琐碎，很容易忘。不过这仅仅是辅助中的辅助，用途有限，平时还是要在平板上做笔记，也要适当做题

Chapter 2

  1. Revenue - Expense = Income (2.2)
  2. Net working capital (NWC) = Current assets - Current liabilities (2.4)
  3. Operating cash flow (OCF) = EBIT + Depreciation - Taxes (2.5)
  4. Capital spending 1 = Purchase of fixed assets - Sales of fixed assets
  5. Capital spending 2 = (End_fix - Beg_fix) + Depreciation
  6. CF of the firm = OCF - Capital spending - Additions to NWC

Chapter 3

  1. 流动性ratio

\[ \colorbox{yellow}{\text{Current ratio}} = \frac{\text{Current assets}}{\text{Current liabilities}}\tag{3.2} \]

\[ \colorbox{yellow}{\text{Quick ratio}} = \frac{\text{Current assets}-\color{red}{\text{Inventory}}}{\text{Current liabilities}} \]

\[ \colorbox{yellow}{\text{Cash ratio}} = \frac{\color{red}{\text{Cash}}}{\text{Current liabilities}} \]

  1. 杠杆ratio

\[ \colorbox{yellow}{\text{Total debt ratio}} = \frac{\text{Total assets}- \text{Total equity}}{\text{Total assets}}\tag{3.2} \]

\[ \colorbox{yellow}{\text{Debt/Equity ratio}} = \frac{\text{Total debt}}{\text{Total equity}} \]

\[ \colorbox{yellow}{\text{Equity multiplier}} = \frac{\text{Total assets}}{\text{Total equity}}=1+\colorbox{pink}{\text{Debt/Equity ratio}} \]

  1. 偿债ratio

\[ \text{Times interest earned}=\frac{EBIT}{\text{Interest}} \]

\[ \text{Cash coverage}=\frac{EBIT+\text{Depreciation}+\text{Amortiztion}}{\text{Interest}} \]

  1. 存货ratio

\[ \text{Inventory turnover}=\frac{\color{red}{COGS}}{\text{Inventory}} \]

\[ \text{Days' sales in inventory}=\frac{365}{\text{Inventory turnover}} \]

  1. 应收ratio

\[ \text{Receivables turnover}=\frac{\color{red}{\text{Sales}}}{\text{Accounts receivables}} \]

\[ \text{Days' sales in receivables}=\frac{365}{\text{Receivables turnover}} \]

  1. 总资产turnover

\[ \text{Total asset turnover}=\frac{\text{Sales}}{\text{Total assets}} \]

  1. 盈利能力measures

\[ \text{Profit margin}=\frac{\text{Net income}}{\text{Sales}} \]

\[ ROA=\frac{\text{Net income}}{\text{Total assets}} \]

\[ ROE=\frac{\text{Net income}}{\text{Total equity}} \]

\[ EBITDA \text{ margin} = \frac{EBITDA}{\text{Sales}} \]

  1. 市值measure

\[ \text{Market capitalization}=\$\text{price}\times \#\text{share} \] \[ PE \text{ ratio}=\frac{\text{price/s}}{\text{earnings/s}} \]

\[ \text{mkt-to-book ratio}=\frac{\text{mkt value/s}}{\text{book value/s}} \]

\[ \text{Enterprise value (EV)}=\text{Market capitalization} + \text{Mkt value of interest bearing debt}-\text{Cash} \]

\[ \text{EV multiple} = \frac{\text{EV}}{\text{EBITDA}} \]

杜邦分析

\[ \text{ROE}=\frac{\text{Net income}}{\text{Total equity}}=\frac{\text{NI}}{\text{TA}}\times \frac{\text{TA}}{\text{TE}}=\text{ROA}\times\text{EM} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \text{ROE}&=\frac{\text{NI}}{\text{Sales}}\times \frac{\text{Sales}}{\text{TA}}\times \frac{\text{TA}}{\text{TE}}\\ &=\text{Profit margin}\times \text{Total asset turnover}\times\text{Equity multiple} \end{aligned} \]
